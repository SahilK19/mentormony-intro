About Us

We at MentorMony believes that you never stop learning in life and there is a student in every one of us. MentorMony is a platform to connect the students with the mentors of their interested field who will help them to achieve their goals. MentorMony also allows everyone to be a mentor and guide others to the road of success.

Benefits for Mentee

Mentee is a person who will be using this platform to connect to individuals who are professional in those streams in which the user wants to master itself. Following are some of the many benefits Mentee will be able to avail from the Mentor.

  • Career Guidance
  • Resume Screening
  • Phone Calls
  • Chat Option
  • Match Making

We are sure that the above mentioned features will help you get a headstart in working towards your career goal with proper career guidance available along with services such as scheduled phone calls from the mentor side. The Mentormony platform will help you in connecting with mentors in those fields in which you aspire to launch your career. Thus, providing a perfect match-making. Hence, This will help you get in touch with professionals and seek advice from them to get to a position where they are but with a headstart, so that you don't do the mistakes which they might have done at starting of their carrer.

Benefits for Mentor

  • Expand your Network

    Networking is a key element of growth and advancement of your profession. Mentoring young talent in the industry creates the ability to grow your own network while enabling your mentees to grow

  • Improving Supervisory Skills

    Successful managers and leaders must be able to develop strong, trustworthy ties. Working with a mentee, particularly anyone outside of your own company, offers the chance to practice the skills available, such as empathy and active listening

  • Encourage self-reflection

    Mentoring gives a chance not only to learn of what you have accomplished but also about how you have come about it. Asking a mentee questions also offers you a greater perspective into your own learning journey and accomplishments.

  • Self satisfaction

    Helping others in tasks in which they are facing any difficulty always gives us a different kind of satisfaction. Helpng others reach a position where you have been or you are in way so that the process becomes a little smoother for them will boost your self-satisfaction.

Senior as a Mentor

MentorMony connects the college students, especially freshmen and sophomore year students, them with a college senior who can clear their doubts regarding internship, placements, research etc. In short a Senior will help a mentee in deciding the steps to reach a goal at ground level , the goal which a mentor has told mentee he/she needs to achive so as to achieve a bigger career goal.
